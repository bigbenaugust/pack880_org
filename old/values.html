Values

We hold fast to the values of our charter organization, the Chapel Hill Bible Church, as well as the core values of Cub Scouting. First and foremost among these are the following:

Faith
Having inner strength and confidence based on trust in Jesus Christ.
Citizenship
Contributing service and showing responsibility to local, state, and national communities
Health
Being personally committed to keeping our minds and bodies clean and fit
Cooperation
Being helpful and working together with others toward a common goal
