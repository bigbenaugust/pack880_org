Pack 880 is seeking rising Kindergarten through 5th grade boys for the 2017-2018 scouting year. We invite visitors to join us for one or more of the following fun events:

There will be plenty of opportunities to join us. Please contact us and ask to be placed on our mailing list to receive announcements for events that suit your schedule.

Our complete calendar is available on request. Our activities for the 2017-2018 scouting year include the following:

Bi-weekly Meetings
Pack 880 will meet regularly on Tuesday nights beginning on Aug. 29, 2017 at the Bible Church for fun and progress towards each rank
Camping Trips
The pack is planning two pack-wide overnight camping trips to Falls Lake (October) and Jordan Lake (April 2018)
Hikes
Take a hike with Pack 880 in November
Pinewood Derby
Boys design, cut, build, and race cars and compete for speed and craftsmanship within the pack and the larger community (February 2018)
Service
Christmas caroling at nursing homes, garbage pick-up on trails, and other opportunities as they arise

…plus a variety of other activities specialized for each age group.

 Congratulations to our scouts for winning 1st and 3rd place at the 2015 Shakori District Pinewood Derby!!
