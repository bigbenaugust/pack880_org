Annual Calendar of Events

There are plenty of opportunities to join us. Please contact us and ask to be placed on our mailing list to receive announcements for events that suit your schedule.

Regular pack and den meetings are held twice a month between August and May at Chapel Hill Bible Church.

Our complete calendar is available on request. Our activities for the 2017-2018 scouting year include the following:

August
  • Informational meeting for prospective scouts and parents
September
  • Twice-monthly pack/den meetings start on 11 Sep 2018 for scouts to work on rank requirements and electives. These are held at CHBC.
  • Pack hike
October
  • Pack participates in Big Sweep creek cleanup project
  • Pack camping trip to Camp Durant, BSA (Carthage, NC)
November
  • Operation Christmas Child shoebox meeting during regular pack/den meeting
  • Pack hike
December
  • Caroling at nursing home
January
  • Pack ice skating
February
  • Pinewood Derby
March
  • Pack hike
April
  • Pack camping trip to Jordan Lake State Park
  • Informational meeting for prospective scouts and parents
May
  • Last pack/den meeting
  • End of Year Party at Falls Lake State Park
Summer
  • Over the summer, Cub Scouts from across the Shakori District (Orange/Chatham counties) participate in activities together.
There are additional activities during the year for some of the dens.

Pinewood Derby in February is a favorite of all the boys!
For more information, please contact our cubmaster.
